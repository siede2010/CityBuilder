This is a Game Focused on showing Multible World Problems at once
in a fun and challanging environment.
The following World Problems are in this game : 2. No Food 11. Sustainable City's 8. Decent Work & Economics 13. Climat Change (Natural Disasters) ...
Goal of the Game :
Uphold a town for 4 minutes while mother nature ravages around you.

Categorys : each slot in the Selection is a Category. the following category's exist
  • Security [Mostly HealthCare and Streets]
  • Nature [Mostly Nature and Happyness]
  • Housing [Mostly Population]
  • Buisniss [Mostly Food & Energy]
  • Happy [Mostly Happyness]
